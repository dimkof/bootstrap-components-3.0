Usage

Auto Tooltips is an enhancement function of Tooltip. Through autotooltip to open & close it. Auto Tooltip will enable all of the tooltip element with user's mouse hover. After close it, tooltip without hover will not be initialized.

Opt-in functionality

For performance reasons, the Auto Tooltip are opt-in, meaning it's closed by default and you need call to open it explicitly.

Methods

.autotooltip(option)

Open & close autotooltip. Accepts a boolean option.

// enable auto tooltip
$.autotooltip(true);

// disable auto tooltip
$.autotooltip(false);
